# Day 1 - CRM Basics

## 1. What is CRM?

CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is a system used by companies to manage customer data, sales, support, and communication in one place.

It helps businesses improve customer relationships and increase sales efficiency.

---

## 2. Why Companies Use Salesforce

Companies use Salesforce because it provides:
- Cloud-based CRM
- Customer data management
- Sales tracking
- Automation tools
- Reports and dashboards
- Customer support management

Salesforce helps businesses improve productivity and customer experience.

---

## 3. Core Salesforce Concepts

### Account
Represents a company or organization.

Example:
TCS, Infosys, Amazon

### Contact
Represents a person associated with an Account.

Example:
Employee or customer details.

### Opportunity
Represents a potential sales deal.

Example:
A company planning to purchase software services.

---

## 4. Real-World Mapping

| Real World | Salesforce |
|---|---|
| Company | Account |
| Employee/Customer | Contact |
| Business Deal | Opportunity |

Example:
- Amazon → Account
- Ramesh HR Manager → Contact
- Software Purchase Deal → Opportunity
